About me

Full Stack Developer with experience in Java and supporting technologies including Maven, WiX Toolset, Jackson, JAXB and more. Past projects have included work for NATO/Canadian Army with Rheinmetall Defence, C/C++ embedded system work for CMC Electronics on the C130T Aircraft CDU and next generation software flight management system for consumer aircrafts.

Outside of my professional career I strive to be as invested in my hobbies as I am in my job. I spend the majority of weekends skiing, training for the next triathlon, or completing crossfit workouts. In my down time, I've been completing this personal project here at TaylorHavart.dev.

This website is a personal project of mine and an outlet for my creative side. Future plans include Kubernetes/Docker integration, and continuation of the existing Django/Python Framework that this website has been created on. Feel free to ask any questions on the progress or throw out a suggestion below. If you wish to keep up to date on the project, take a look at my github here.
